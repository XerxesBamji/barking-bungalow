The Barking Bungalow

Welcome to The Bark Bungalow. Here at The Bark Bungalow your dog isn't a guest, they're family. We offer a fun, loving safe home-from-home experience for your furry companions based in Sully, The Vale Of Glamorgan. We have a detached bungalow with a large garden situated 2 minutes from Sully beach.
