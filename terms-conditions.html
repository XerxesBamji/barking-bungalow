Terms & Conditions

  1. Services Provided

    We offer the following services:

    • Home Dog Boarding: Overnight stays in a home environment.
    • Dog Walking: Regular or one-off walks tailored to your dog's needs.
    • Daycare: Supervised daytime care. (Subject to availability)

  2. Requirements for Dogs

    To ensure the safety and well-being of all dogs in our care, all dogs must:

    • Be fully vaccinated (proof required).
    • Be up-to-date on flea, tick, and worm treatments.
    • Be non-aggressive and able to socialize safely with other dogs and humans.
    • Be house-trained (for boarding).
    • Be microchipped and wear an ID tag.

    We reserve the right to refuse service if your dog does not meet these requirements or behaves aggressively.

  3. Meet & Greet / Trial stage.

    • We require a meet and greet walk in sully for every dog. After the walk we will show to our house and setup.
    • If the initial meet goes well and you are happy. We then require a trial night (morning drop off - following morning pick up) to assess your dog in details and get him/her used to us, our house and routines. If this goes well we will be happy to have your dog.

  4. Bookings & Payment

    • All bookings must be confirmed in advance.
    • For long stays a 50% deposit is required to secure the booking.
    • Full payment is due 4 weeks before the boarding begins (or on the day of service).
    • Accepted payment methods: cash or bank transfer.

  5. Cancellations & Refunds

    • Cancellations made 7 days or more in advance will receive a full refund.
    • Cancellations within 7 days of the start date will incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the total amount of the whole booking.
    • No refunds for early return during a boarding stay.No refunds for early return during a boarding stay.

  6. Emergencies & Veterinary Care

    In the event of illness or emergency:

    • We will make every effort to contact you or your emergency contact.
    • If urgent, we will take your dog to your named vet or to the Vale Veterinary Centre in Barry who we have set up a relationship with.
    • You are responsible for any vet fees incurred during your dog's stay/walk.

  7. Liability

    • We will take all reasonable care of your dog(s).
    • However, we are not liable for injury, loss, illness, or death unless caused by proven negligence.
    • You agree to be responsible for any damage or injury your dog causes to property, people, or other animals.

  8. Photos & Updates

    • Here at the bark bungalow we take pride in our customers feedback about the amount of footage and updates we send whilst your dog is in our care. We do sent lots of updates every day without fail.
    • We may take photos/videos of your dog during their stay or walks for updates or promotional use (social media, website).
    • Please inform us in writing if you do not consent to this.

  9. Drop-off & Pick-up

    • We will be in touch to arrange a drop off and collection time for your dog. We understand sometimes plans change so please let us know if you as soon as possible if you are late.
    • As previously mentioned our dog boarding rate is for 24 hours. We allow 2 hours free but any further hours we charge a day care rate. For instance: if you drop off your dog at 9am on the 1st of June and collect and collect at 6pm on the 2nd of June. You will be charge 1 night for boarding plus 1 night if day care.

  10. Keys and Access (For Walking Clients)

    • If you provide a key for walking services, it will be securely stored.
    • Keys will only be used for accessing your dog and will not be copied or shared.
    • If you have a lock box then let us know the code.

  11. Agreement

    We will send you a form to fill at the meet and greet or trial stage. We require signatures that you agree to these terms and conditions.
